RMS (Rental Management System) is a system developed to ease communication between tenants and property managers.

Key features of RMS:

  • Allows tenants to submit maintenance requests online
  • Enables property managers to manage lease agreements and rental payments
  • Provides a platform for tenants to give feedback and communicate with property managers
  • Facilitates efficient communication and coordination between tenants and property managers

RMS aims to streamline the rental management process, improve tenant satisfaction, and enhance property management efficiency.
